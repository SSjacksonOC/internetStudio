Fox:

 With nary a sigh—just chuckle—nor tear—just blood—to wet the ground, the proud Fox finishes looking around. Not a soul dared to disturb it. Not a quick peep or peek did peel from the scene.
The Fox had done away with Rats well-enough it seems. "One mistake is one mistake too many, although I forgot your severance pay." said the Fox as it tore
off the two blue wings. Caching the employee for later, the Fox leaved.

There is nothing left to see...
